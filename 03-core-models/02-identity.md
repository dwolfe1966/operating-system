# Identity

> "Identity is not the person.
>
> Identity is our current representation of the person."

---

# Purpose

This document defines our current model of Identity.

Identity is the central domain object of the Identity Operating System.

Everything the company builds ultimately exists to improve the quality of our understanding of identity.

Identity is therefore not simply data.

It is the evolving representation of a human being.

---

# Core Question

What is identity?

---

# Working Model

Identity is an evolving representation of an individual constructed from available evidence.

Identity is not the person.

Identity is our current best model of the person.

Because every model is incomplete,

identity is necessarily incomplete.

Identity improves as evidence improves.

Identity evolves as reality evolves.

---

# Why This Model Exists

Humans never interact directly with another person's complete reality.

We interact with representations.

Names.

Faces.

Reputations.

Stories.

Public records.

Professional history.

Relationships.

Digital footprints.

Memory.

Every one of these contributes to our model of another person.

Identity therefore exists in the intersection between reality and representation.

---

# Properties of Identity

Identity is...

• Incomplete

No representation captures an entire person.

---

• Evolving

People change.

Identity changes.

Representations should evolve accordingly.

---

• Evidence-Based

Identity becomes stronger as evidence accumulates.

---

• Probabilistic

Identity is rarely certain.

Confidence should always be represented.

---

• Relational

Identity exists within networks of people, places, organizations and events.

---

• Contextual

Different contexts reveal different aspects of identity.

Professional.

Personal.

Legal.

Social.

Historical.

---

# Product Implications

IDLookup should not think in terms of public records.

It should think in terms of identities.

Public records become evidence.

Addresses become events.

Phones become relationships.

Employment becomes history.

Everything contributes to a richer identity representation.

---

# Identity Is A Living Model

The platform should continuously refine identities as new evidence becomes available.

Identity is therefore not a report.

Identity is a living model.

This naturally leads to:

- monitoring
- alerts
- AI summaries
- confidence updates
- timeline evolution

Identity Intelligence becomes continuous rather than transactional.

---

# Identity Maps

An Identity Map is one possible representation of identity.

Rather than exposing disconnected records,

the platform organizes information into a coherent model.

Possible dimensions include:

• Timeline

• Relationships

• Confidence

• Life Events

• Public Activity

• Evidence

• Sources

Different representations may be appropriate for different users and tasks.

---

# AI Implications

Artificial Intelligence should refine identity models.

Not merely summarize records.

AI should:

- identify relationships

- reconcile conflicting evidence

- estimate confidence

- explain uncertainty

- identify significant change

- surface patterns

The objective is a continually improving identity model.

---

# Evaluation

When evaluating identity representations ask:

Does this improve our understanding of the individual?

Does this better explain available evidence?

Does it distinguish evidence from inference?

Does it communicate uncertainty honestly?

Does it improve future decision making?

---

# Open Questions

How should conflicting identities be represented?

Can multiple valid identities coexist?

Should confidence exist at the identity level or the evidence level?

How should identities split or merge over time?

How should AI communicate uncertainty?

What aspects of identity should remain intentionally unmodeled?

---

# Why This Matters

Traditional people-search products organize information around databases.

Identity Intelligence organizes information around people.

This distinction changes nearly every downstream design decision.

Search becomes identity discovery.

Reports become identity representations.

Monitoring becomes identity evolution.

Artificial Intelligence becomes identity refinement.

Identity is therefore not simply another model within the Operating System.

It is the central object around which the product is built.
