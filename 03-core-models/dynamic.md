# Dynamics

> "Ontology describes what exists.
>
> Dynamics describe how it changes."

---

# Purpose

The ontology defines the structure of our world.

Dynamics define its behavior.

Objects alone do not produce understanding.

Objects influence one another over time.

Dynamics describe those interactions.

The Identity Operating System uses dynamics to explain how observations become evidence, how evidence changes beliefs, how understanding evolves, and how better decisions emerge.

Without dynamics, ontology is static.

Without ontology, dynamics have nothing to act upon.

Together they explain the system.

---

# Core Question

How do things influence one another?

---

# Working Model

Every meaningful change inside the Identity Operating System occurs through one or more fundamental dynamics.

These dynamics describe how information moves through the system and how people's internal models evolve.

The purpose of the Operating System is not merely to model reality.

It is to understand the dynamics by which understanding improves.

---

# Current Candidate Dynamics

The following dynamics represent our current understanding.

They are intentionally provisional.

They should evolve as our understanding improves.

## Observation

Reality becomes observable.

---

## Interpretation

Observations become evidence.

---

## Influence

Evidence changes internal models.

---

## Learning

Internal models evolve.

---

## Understanding

Understanding emerges.

---

## Decision

Understanding influences decisions.

---

## Action

Decisions create actions.

---

## Feedback

Actions produce new observations.

The cycle repeats.

# The Learning Loop

```
Reality

↓

Observation

↓

Interpretation

↓

Evidence

↓

Influence

↓

Internal Model

↓

Understanding

↓

Decision

↓

Action

↓

Reality
```

This loop represents our current working model of how people improve their understanding of reality.

Identity Intelligence exists to improve this cycle.

# Why Dynamics Matter

Most software products focus on objects.

We believe competitive advantage increasingly comes from understanding dynamics.

Data is static.

Reasoning is dynamic.

Identity is dynamic.

Trust is dynamic.

Confidence is dynamic.

Understanding is dynamic.

Our platform should therefore optimize the dynamics of learning rather than simply storing information.

---

# Product Implications

Search is not retrieval.

Search begins a learning process.

AI is not text generation.

AI accelerates understanding.

Monitoring is not notifications.

Monitoring continuously updates internal models.

Reports are not documents.

Reports are dynamic representations.

Everything becomes part of an evolving learning system.

---

# AI Implications

Artificial Intelligence should participate in the dynamics of learning.

Its responsibilities include:

- organizing observations

- interpreting evidence

- influencing understanding

- explaining reasoning

- identifying uncertainty

- recommending additional evidence

AI therefore becomes a participant in cognition rather than a replacement for cognition.

---

# Evaluation

A proposed dynamic belongs in the Identity Operating System if:

- it explains repeated behavior

- it influences multiple downstream decisions

- it improves understanding

- it remains useful independent of implementation

- it can be observed within the product
